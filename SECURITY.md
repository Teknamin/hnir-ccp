# Security Policy

## Supported Versions

We are committed to providing security updates for the latest major version of HNIR-CCP.

| Version | Supported          |
| ------- | ------------------ |
| 1.x.x   | :white_check_mark: |

## Reporting a Vulnerability

The HNIR-CCP team and community take all security vulnerabilities seriously. Thank you for improving the security of our project. We appreciate your efforts and responsible disclosure and will make every effort to acknowledge your contributions.

To report a security vulnerability, please email the project lead at `aravind@raviaravind.com`.

We will respond to your report within 48 hours and will work with you to understand and resolve the issue. We will not disclose the vulnerability until we have a fix in place.
