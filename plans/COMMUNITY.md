# Welcome to the HNIR-CCP Community!

We are thrilled that you are interested in the Human-in-the-Loop Conversation Control Plane (HNIR-CCP) project. We believe in the power of community and welcome contributions from everyone.

This document outlines how you can get involved and where to find us.

## Communication Channels

The best way to engage with the community and the development team is through our official communication channels:

*   **GitHub Discussions:** For questions, feature proposals, and general discussions, please use our [GitHub Discussions page](https://github.com/Teknamin/hnir-ccp/discussions). This is the primary place for community interaction.

## How to Contribute

We welcome contributions of all kinds, not just code. Here are some of the ways you can help:

*   **Improve Documentation:** If you find a typo, a confusing sentence, or an area where the documentation could be better, please let us know or submit a pull request.
*   **Report a Bug:** If you find a bug, please create a detailed bug report in our [GitHub Issues](https://github.com/Teknamin/hnir-ccp/issues).
*   **Suggest a Feature:** Have a great idea for a new feature? We'd love to hear it. Please start a discussion on our [GitHub Discussions page](https://github.com/Teknamin/hnir-ccp/discussions).
*   **Share Your Work:** If you are using HNIR-CCP in your own project, we would love to hear about it. Please share it with us in the "Show and Tell" category on our discussions page.

## Code of Conduct

We are committed to fostering a welcoming and inclusive community. All community members are expected to adhere to our [Code of Conduct](CODE_OF_CONDUCT.md). Please be respectful and considerate of others.

We look forward to collaborating with you!
